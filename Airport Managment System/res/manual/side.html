Side on View

The right-hand side of the screen will contain the runway visualisation panels, including the top-down and side-on views. The side-on view is the visualisation nearest the bottom of the page.

This side-on visualisation gives you a side-on of the runway, as if you were viewing it from alongside.

Navigation

In order to navigate around this view, translate from side to side.

Translation

In order to translate the view, you simply LEFT click somewhere upon the side-on view and drag the mouse in the direction you wish to move the view in.
The side-on view only allows you to shift the view in a horizontal direction.

Action Bar

A convenient feature provided by views is the "action bar". To bring up the action bar, simply press and release the mouse anywhere within the view (do not drag the mouse).
This will bring up a set of icons, each with an assosiated task.

Paint bucket: toggle visualisation texturing

Clicking upon the paint bucket icon will toggle the mode of this visualisation between "coloured" and "textured".
The applications default mode is textured.

Expand: toggle full screen mode

Clicking upon the expand button icon will toggle between fullscreen and non-fullscreen modes. This is useful for getting a more detailed look at the side-on view.
Please note, to get out of fullscreen mode, you must repeat the process of clicking the expand icon.
